How to Play Shape-Cast

Game Objective

Reach the target score within the time limit by strategically placing shapes.

Shape Values

  • Circle: 5 points
  • Square: 10 points
  • Triangle: 15 points
  • Diamond: -1 point

Gameplay

  • Press 'Start Game' to begin a new game
  • Click any shape button to select it
  • Click on the canvas to place the selected shape
  • Once you have selected a shape, you do not need to click the same shape button again unless you would like to change the shape.
  • Use 'Clear Space' to remove all shapes (WARNING: Your score will reset!)
  • Press 'Start Game' to begin a new game or restart if you are stuck

Tips

  • Plan your choice of shapes carefully
  • Watch the timer
  • Use diamonds strategically to adjust your score
